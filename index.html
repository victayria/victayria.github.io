Intro

Data Analyst, Professional Development and Education Presenter. Passionate about harnessing the power of technology to help others learn and companies thrive. Experience with Excel/Tableau/SQL/Python to glean actionable insights from a wide variety of data sources.
I love a project that challenges me to research new ways of solving problems. Over the past five years, I have enjoyed the greatest challenge of my life so far: teaching 7th graders to love math. It was during this time that I developed my love of technology, patterns and data analysis.
I enrolled in the Thinkful Data Analytics program in September 2019 to further challenge myself in using technology for data analysis projects. I am looking forward to changing careers so that my talents in this field may be fostered and cultivated.
